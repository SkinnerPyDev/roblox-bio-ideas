## What is BioBlox?

[BioBlox](https://bioblox.fun/) is a free web tool built for Roblox players who want a unique, stylish profile bio without the hassle of writing one from scratch. It generates 100,000+ unique Roblox bio combinations tailored to your character style, personality, and favorite RP game.

No signup. No limits. 100% free.

---

## Features

### 🎭 Roblox Bio Generator
- 10 unique styles: Cute, Emo, Anime, Aesthetic, Preppy, Gamer, Cottagecore, Y2K, Grunge, Baddie
- 10 personality types: Shy, Savage, Soft, Mysterious, Chill, Funny, Flirty, Toxic, Wholesome, Edgy
- Game-specific bios for Brookhaven, Bloxburg, Berry Avenue, Da Hood, Adopt Me, Royal High and more
- 3 bio lengths: One Liner, Short, Long
- Pronoun support: He/Him, She/Her, Custom
- 7 decorative border styles
- One-click copy to clipboard
- Real Roblox profile preview with your username

### 🃏 Roblox Flex Card Generator
- Enter your Roblox username and generate a stunning avatar card
- 30+ card themes: Ocean, Fire, Galaxy, Neon, Bubblegum, Stardust and more
- Shows your flex score, drip level, followers and rarest items
- Download as high-quality PNG
- Share directly to TikTok, Discord, Instagram, Reddit and X
